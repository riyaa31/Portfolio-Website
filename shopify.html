Clothing Website - ZRC

This is a personal project created by me during quaratine. ZRC Clothing

ZRC Clothing is an online shopping website for both male and female, along with a few accessories and all sort of clothes. From women's dresses to men's winter coats.

The Refund and Cancellations policy have also been implemented. There are various methods of payment available to our customers including AMEX, Google Pay, Apple Pay, PayPal, Mastercard, Visa etc.

The logo ZRC was designed by me using PhotoPea.

Technologies:
  • - Ruby
  • - PhotoPea
  • - Ruby on Rails
  • - Shopify Platform
